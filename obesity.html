Obesity Surgery

Our weight loss surgery program includes Extensive pre-operative evaluation and preparation
• Psychological evaluation
• Pre and post-op behavior modification program run by our psychologist
• weekly support groups
• Personal trainer Diet counseling with our registered dietician
• Life long follow up

apex

Apex Hospital is an OSSI (Obesity Surgery Society of India) recognized surgery center in India.
Dr. Sachin Jhawar, MS, MRCS (Edinburgh ,UK), AFRCS (Dublin, Ireland), EITS (Diploma in Tele Surgery, Strasbourg, France) advanced laparoscopic and bariatric surgeon, head of the obesity center, has treated patients ranging from 18 years to 66 years in age.


is it so easy?

For Surgery, patients have to stay in hospital for 2-7 days. Weight loss starts within the month after surgery and patients does not put on weight once it is reduced. Surgery does not cause any weakness and patient can get back to his normal routine within 15 days post surgery. Post surgery a lot of patient can get rid of the problem of diabetes forever. This surgery is also effective in combating stress, cardiac problems, joint pain etc
