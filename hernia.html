Minimal Invasive Joint Replacement Surgery

Joint Replacement Surgeries – including knee, hip, shoulder and elbow replacements, are our forte. Across our hospitals, our surgeon has done more 1,000 joint replacement procedures. The Joint Replacement Centre, an initiative of Apex Hospital has done pioneering work in offering patients state of the art treatment options such as implantation of patient-specific devices, and Minimally Invasive Knee & Hip Replacement. So whether it’s Poly Trauma Surgery, Revision Surgery, Minimally Invasive Arthroplasty Surgery or rehabilitation you need, rest assured our Bone & Joint specialists, including surgeons, sports medicine specialists, rheumatologists, nurses, physical and occupational therapists, will do whatever it takes to get you back to leading a full, active, independent life.
